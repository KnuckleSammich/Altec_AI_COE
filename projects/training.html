Training Request System

Overview

An end-to-end training request system that captures, routes, and tracks training needs across the plant. Supervisors and associates submit requests through a simple form; Power Automate handles routing, approvals, and notifications.

Key Capabilities

  • Standardized intake form for all training requests
  • Automated routing to the appropriate trainer or department
  • Approval workflows integrated with Teams notifications
  • Centralized request log for reporting and follow-up

Impact

Replaced ad-hoc emails and informal requests with a structured, transparent process. Improved trainer workload visibility, accelerated response times, and gave leadership clear data on training demand across the plant.
