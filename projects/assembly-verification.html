Overview

Designed and deployed a structured verification process for assembly parts, providing welders with consistent, traceable feedback on the parts they produce and the parts they receive from upstream operations.

11,000+ Submissions

Key Capabilities

  • Structured form for verifying part quality and conformance
  • Direct feedback loop to upstream welders and operators
  • Improved accountability across assembly cells
  • Historical data supports root-cause analysis for recurring issues
