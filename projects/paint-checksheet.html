Overview

Replaced the paper-based paint system check sheet with a digital, mobile-friendly version that captures structured data on every paint job. Enables full traceability of jobs, fluid usage rates, and process conditions across every shift.

10,000+ Submissions

Key Capabilities

  • Tracks jobs, paint codes, and fluid rates per shift
  • Built-in validation prevents incomplete or invalid submissions
  • Historical data accessible in Power BI for trending and audits
  • Mobile-friendly for use on the paint floor
