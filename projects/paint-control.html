Overview

Built statistical process control (SPC) charts to monitor critical paint kitchen parameters in real time, including viscosity readings and temperature. The charts give the paint team early warning of drift before it impacts finish quality.

7,000+ Submissions

Key Capabilities

  • Real-time tracking of viscosity and temperature readings
  • Automated control limit calculations
  • Visual dashboards highlighting out-of-control conditions
  • Historical data analysis for continuous improvement
