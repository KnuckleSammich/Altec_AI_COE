Overview

Designed and deployed a streamlined hazardous waste removal process using QR codes and automated Teams notifications. The system ensures environmental compliance by providing a fast, traceable way for associates to request hazardous waste pickup from anywhere on the plant floor.

630+ Responses

How It Works

  • QR codes posted at hazardous waste collection points
  • Associate scans → opens a Microsoft Form on their device
  • Form submission triggers a Power Automate flow
  • Automated Teams message routed to the responsible team for pickup
  • All submissions logged for compliance reporting
